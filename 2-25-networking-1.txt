IPv4 IPv6
    We are running out of IPv4 addresses

TCP UDP
    TCP slower, but better for data correctness
    UDP faster, but no promise of delivery

Switch Router
    Switch handles sending data between devices on LAN
    Router is smarter, has some software to protect devices from WAN
